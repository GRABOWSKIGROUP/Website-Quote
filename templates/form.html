How to use this quote tool

Choose the package that best matches the size of the website, then add any special features you need. If you are unsure, pick the closest fit—the quote is designed to give an easy starting estimate, not overwhelm you with technical jargon.

  • Tier = overall size and complexity of the site.
  • Forms = contact, adoption, intake, volunteer, or application forms.
  • E-commerce = online payments, checkout, donations, or product sales.
  • Backend/Admin = a login area where your team can add, edit, or remove content.
  • Integrations = connections to outside tools like CRMs, email platforms, calendars, or payment systems.

Quick recommendation guide

  • Pick Flat static for a simple brochure-style site with basic pages.
  • Pick Mid tier for a business site with more structure and a few interactive features.
  • Pick Detailed / full featured when the site needs logins, dashboards, advanced content management, payments, or custom workflows.

Tier Differences

Flat static

Starts at $750

Best for a simple online presence. Think home page, about page, services, gallery, and contact info with very little ongoing editing.

Mid tier

Starts at $1,200

Best for a growing business or organization that needs more pages, stronger layout planning, and a few interactive pieces like forms or integrations.

Detailed / full featured

Starts at $2,500

Best for custom workflows and management features. Use this when you need online donations, user-editable content, dashboards, listings, or more advanced functionality.
